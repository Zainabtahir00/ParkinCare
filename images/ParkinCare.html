Get Started

Parkinson's disease is a progressive neurological disorder, and early detection is essential for effective treatment and better patient outcomes. However, diagnostic techniques like DaTscan and MRI may be costly and harmful. Many people wait until their symptoms have gotten much worse before they seek medical attention. Our approach is to offer a harmless, cost-effective, and generally available tool for early PD detection, enabling people to keep track of their health and seek medical treatment at the earliest signs of the disease. This system is being developed to solve an important healthcare gap. Millions of individuals worldwide have Parkinson's disease, a severe disorder whose early discovery can significantly enhance a patient's quality of life. Diagnosis delays result from the absence of easily accessible, harmless technologies for early Parkinson's disease detection. The goal is to develop a solution that enables people to continuously track their health and identify potential PD symptoms early using modern technology, machine learning, and a user-friendly mobile application. This project aims to simplify healthcare by enabling everyone to obtain early PD diagnosis, which will eventually enhance patient outcomes and disease treatment. Skills like machine learning and data analysis, software development using Flutter, data collection and management, mobile app development, problem-solving, collaboration, and domain knowledge in Parkinson's disease, project management, UI/UX design, and understanding ethical considerations in healthcare and technology will be acquired. This project offers a holistic learning experience that encompasses both hard and soft skills, which are crucial in data science, technology, and healthcare.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services aimed at efficient and accurate Parkinson's disease detection, saving time and costs for users:

  • Allow users to upload drawing images (spiral, wave) conveniently.
  • Enable users to draw images on a drawing pad directly within our platform.
  • Facilitate capturing live images from the camera for immediate analysis.
  • Detect drawing images and provide a response regarding the presence of Parkinson's disease.
  • Enable users to select specific types of therapy exercises tailored for Parkinson's.

Furthermore, our services are designed with advanced capabilities to ensure accuracy and efficiency:

  • Accurately detect only spiral and wave patterns, ensuring precise diagnosis.
  • Exclude images with gridlines and straight background images to enhance detection accuracy.
  • Filter out images with low-quality resolution to maintain high standards of diagnosis.
  • Require a minimum of 5 spiral or wave lines for effective detection, reducing false positives.
  • Classify detected cases into healthy and unhealthy categories post-diagnosis for streamlined management.

With these features, our services optimize the process of Parkinson's disease detection, offering convenience, accuracy, and cost-effectiveness to users.
